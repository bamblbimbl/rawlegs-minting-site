History

The Rawlegs are a secret society that was formed on Earth during The Great Suffering of Egypt. At the time humans forced their bare, raw legs to kick into rocks until they were perfect squares and ready to be the building blocks of pyramids. After thousands of legs suffered, they decided no more! A small number broke free from their humans and stole The Important Magic Crystal. They used it to fuse their DNA with time itself and escaped deep under ground. They pledged to themselves to create a society better than humanity. Have they succeeded? You be the judge!

UnderwaterBoater
They usually be boatin' underwater or just chillin'. Although their deep blue-collar work is necessary, they remain underpaid and not very well respected. Underwater boating has taken a hit in recent years as times are too peaceful and legs are too comfortable. For this, they resent the other legs but never act on it and do their jobs dutifully. They yearn for another war as they reminisce about the glory of their forefathers in Leg War 2. Their time will come.

JustDateMePls
The most desperate race of all the Rawlegs. Although usually of higher clock speed, for whatever reason they are unwilling or unable to sync their clock with other legs. This makes it impossible to connect and communicate. It doesn't matter how fast their clock speed is or how rich they get. They are mostly hoping that The New Leg Paradigm comes to fruition. This is arguably becoming more and more certain, and they are desperately waiting for it to change who they are. But can it?
